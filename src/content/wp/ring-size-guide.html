Ring Size Guide

Find Your Perfect Ring Size

Ensuring the correct ring size is essential for comfort and security when wearing your jewellery. At Minskhi, we want your piece to fit perfectly from the moment it arrives.

If you are unsure of your ring size, you can easily measure it at home using the simple method shown below. Once you have taken your measurement, compare it with the Australian ring size chart to find your correct size.

How to Measure Your Ring Size at Home

You can determine your ring size at home using a piece of string, a thin strip of paper, or a flexible measuring tape.

01

Wrap a piece of string or paper around the base of your finger where the ring will sit.

02

Mark the point where the string or paper overlaps to complete the circle.

03

Lay the string or paper flat and measure the length in millimetres using a ruler.

04

Compare this measurement with the circumference column in the ring size chart below.

Helpful Note

For a visual demonstration, you can search “How to measure your ring size at home” on Google or YouTube. There are many helpful videos available that demonstrate the simple method described above.

These tutorials will guide you step-by-step on how to measure your finger using a piece of string or a strip of paper, allowing you to compare the measurement with the Australian ring size chart provided on this page.

If your measurement falls between two sizes, we generally recommend selecting the larger size for comfort.

Helpful Tips for Accurate Sizing

  • Measure your finger at the end of the day, when it is naturally at its largest.
  • Avoid measuring when your hands are very cold, as fingers may shrink slightly.
  • Ensure the measuring string or paper is snug but not tight around your finger.
  • If you are ordering a wider band, you may prefer a slightly larger size for comfort.

Still Unsure About Your Ring Size?

If you are unsure about your measurement, we recommend booking a private consultation with the Minskhi team. We will be happy to guide you through the sizing process and help ensure your ring fits perfectly before it is crafted.
